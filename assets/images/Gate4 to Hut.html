Route from Gate-4 to Hut

Hut:

  • Start at Gate 4 and walk straight for 20 meters.
  • Turn right and continue for another 20 meters.
  • ake a left and walk for 20 meters, then turn right and walk for 30 meters.
  • Turn left and proceed for 60 meters.
  • Walk straight for 20 meters, take a right and continue for 35 meters.
  • The Hut will be ahead.
